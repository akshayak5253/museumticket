Museum Highlights:
The Guindy Museum, Chennai, or the Madras Museum, is a museum  of human history and culture located in the Government Museum Complex in the neighbourhood of Guindy  in Chennai,India . Started in 1851, it is the second oldest museum in  after the Indian museum  in . It is particularly rich in archaeological and numismatic collections. It has the largest collection of  antiquities outside Europe . Among them, the colossal Museum Theatre is one of the most impressive
